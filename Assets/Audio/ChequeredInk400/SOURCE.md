# Chequered Ink — 400 Sounds Pack

Source: https://ci.itch.io/400-sounds-pack

Selected sounds from the free `400 Sounds Pack` by Chequered Ink are used in
this project. The author permits personal and commercial use without required
credit. The original, unmodified sounds must not be resold or redistributed as
another asset pack.

Imported selection:

- UI: `select_2.wav`, `synth_warning.wav`
- Environment: `door_open.wav`, `door_close.wav`
- Footsteps: `foley_footstep_concrete_1.wav` through
  `foley_footstep_concrete_4.wav`
- Materials: `clothing_thud.wav`
- Weapons: `shot_muffled.wav`
- Other: `whoosh_2.wav`
